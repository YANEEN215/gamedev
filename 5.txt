Rongorongo Texts
To this day, this script remains unreadable

Hailing from the island of Rapa Nui, the Rongorongo texts are another undeciphered language. Written mainly on wooden objects, these texts likely date to between the 17th and 19th centuries. The texts are written in a script made up of hieroglyphic symbols written in alternating directions (in a style called boustrophedon).

Legend has it that the legendary founders of the island brought the writing with them from their homeland, but archaeologists think that this writing is indigenous to Rapa Nui. To this day, the text and its history are shrouded in mystery. No one knows yet what interesting things these texts could reveal about Rapa Nui's history.
